Background of the business

ATB Dry goods is a ukay- ukay and online type of business selling different kind of clothes and other dry goods product like shoes and bags, we are also selling    ukay- ukay in bundles. Established on july 2017 owned by Mr. Harrold Buna and Mrs. Baby Joy T. Buna, ATB is came from our sons name Abiel Tangson Buna & Aiden Tangson Buna.

Vision   

To become one that's have of the most efficient and effective service providers in our town.

Goals and objectives

1. To become the best and the most reliable store in our town
2. Improving the customer satisfaction through the good quality of products
